# Sample Frame

This document defines the sampling logic for the jade trend research project.

## Research Goal

Build a sample set that can answer:

- what jade product directions are already selling well,
- which product forms can support the current core audience,
- which signals can extend into younger growth segments,
- which adjacent-category aesthetics are likely to migrate into jade.

## Target Sample Mix

| Pool | Target Count | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Core jade accounts | 30-50 | Establish category reality and recurring winning patterns |
| Adjacent-category accounts | 20-30 | Surface design signals that may migrate into jade |
| Keyword discovery sets | 40-80 keywords total | Capture both known demand and emerging search language |

## Core Jade Account Classes

Use these classes to avoid overfitting to one kind of creator.

- `commercial_conversion`: accounts that repeatedly show product availability, sales intent, or direct purchase framing
- `aesthetic_editorial`: accounts that build taste and style through strong product photography and design language
- `young_experimental`: accounts that use more modern, playful, layered, or fashion-led jade presentation

Recommended balance:

- 40% commercial conversion
- 35% aesthetic editorial
- 25% young experimental

## Account Size Tiers

To reduce account-size bias, stratify both core-jade and adjacent-category accounts into these tiers:

- `head`: category-leading or clearly high-visibility accounts
- `mid`: consistent operators with meaningful engagement but less category dominance
- `emerging`: smaller or newer accounts with clear product experimentation or fast-rising signals

Recommended balance:

- 25% head
- 45% mid
- 30% emerging

This prevents the sample from drifting too far toward either polished incumbents or small novelty accounts.

## Core Jade Inclusion Criteria

Include accounts that meet most of the following:

- frequent product-oriented posting
- visible product images or clear product video covers
- consistent interaction on product notes
- recent activity within the last 6-12 months
- clear positioning around jade, jade jewelry, or jade styling
- some evidence of price-band relevance to 3000-8000 RMB

Prioritize accounts that show:

- repeated material combinations,
- repeated shape language,
- repeated styling logic,
- repeatable visual systems rather than one-off novelty.

## Core Jade Exclusion Criteria

Do not prioritize accounts that are:

- purely educational with little product expression
- almost entirely reposts without distinct product identity
- too broad across unrelated categories to support jade trend inference
- inactive or stale in the observation window

## Adjacent-Category Account Classes

Use these classes to identify cross-category movement.

- `18k_light_jewelry`: modern gold, diamonds, zircon, and modular fine jewelry
- `pearls`: fashion-forward pearl styling, especially when mixed with metal or color
- `colored_gemstones`: color-led jewelry, playful palettes, or statement gemstones
- `agarwood_eastern_accessories`: agarwood, wood, prayer-like accessories, or Eastern-leaning mixed-material pieces
- `fashion_accessories`: non-jade accessories with strong styling, color, or chain language

Recommended balance:

- 30% 18K gold light jewelry
- 20% pearls
- 20% colored gemstones
- 15% agarwood / Eastern accessories
- 15% fashion accessories

## Adjacent-Category Inclusion Criteria

Include accounts that show at least one of the following:

- strong use of color as an aesthetic driver
- strong chain, layering, or stackable logic
- modern metalwork or sculptural hardware
- clear premium styling that could plausibly translate to jade
- visible wearability, gifting, or collectible framing

## Keyword Discovery Logic

The keyword system should cover three search intents:

- `jade_basics`: direct category demand
- `modern_jade_design`: new forms and style language
- `material_combination`: adjacent materials and hybrid concepts
- `style_and_scene`: wearability, styling, and occasion language
- `price_and_gifting`: purchase intent and gift intent
- `adjacent_categories`: non-jade trend signals

Within `price_and_gifting`, include both exact-price language and real market band language used by jade buyers and sellers, such as:

- `小千`
- `中千`
- `大千`
- `小万`
- `预算5k`
- `预算8k`

## Note Selection Logic

For each source pool, prioritize notes with:

- high likes or saves relative to the account baseline
- clear product images
- obvious product differentiation
- comments that show buying intent, questions, or styling interest
- strong cross-product consistency inside the same account

When a note contains multiple products, treat each clearly distinct product motif as a separate analysis unit.

## Working Rule

Image review is mandatory before concluding on a product direction.
If the image cannot be interpreted clearly, mark the sample as incomplete rather than forcing a conclusion.
